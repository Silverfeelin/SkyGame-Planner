Graphs - Spirits

The below graph shows the days it takes for season spirits to return. Spirits that have visited at least once are shown, in order of season.
The return time is the amount of days between the end of the season and the first visit as a Traveling Spirit or a Returning Spirit.

The below graph shows the days since each season spirit has last visited. Spirits are shown in order of season. Spirits in orange have not visited since their season departure.

The below graph shows the cost of each season spirit based on their last visit. Spirits are shown in order of season. The blue bar represents the cost in candles, the red bar represents the cost in hearts.
