The grey number (30) next to each item shows the total friendship points you get from buying the item.
If you can't unlock all the items you want during the season the calculator will help you figure out which items are most efficient to buy.

A simple example:
Buying a 4 (60) blessing is twice as efficient as buying a 4 (30) blessing, since it gives double the friendship points for the same cost.
Buying the first blessing practically saves you 6 days of spirit adventures (6 x 10) whereas buying the second blessing only saves you 3 days (3 x 10).
