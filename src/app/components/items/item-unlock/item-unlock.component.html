Quick start

This page will help you get started with the Sky Planner. It is recommended to have the game open on another device and keep it next to you.

We'll go through your closets to figure out what items you have.
To get started, go to the outfit closet and check how many columns of outfits your game displays.
This will make it easier to compare your closet to the website.
