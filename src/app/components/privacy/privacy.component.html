Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23rd, 2025
Effective: January 23rd, 2025

Welcome to Sky Planner (the "Website"). We value your privacy and want to be transparent about the data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. This privacy policy outlines our practices related to the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our Website.

1. Information We Collect

The Website does not collect any personal data from its users. All data related to your game progress is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is accessible only by you.

Some pages of the Website allow you to submit data and make it available to other users. In these instances, your IP address may be logged to prevent abuse and a randomly generated token is stored alongside the submitted data to allow you to manage your submissions. This token is unidentifiable and cannot be used to track you.

Some pages of the Website require you to authenticate through Discord instead. In these instances the Website exclusively uses your Discord User ID and username; no other data is collected or stored.

All code of the Website, client-side and server-side, is open-source and can be reviewed on the official Sky Planner GitHub repository.

2. Data Storage and Security

Your game progress data is stored locally in your browser's storage. Additionally, you have the option to connect the Website to your Dropbox account to save your progress. When you choose to save your progress to Dropbox, your data is stored privately in your personal Dropbox account. Please review Dropbox's privacy policy for more information on their data handling practices: Dropbox Privacy Policy.

You are responsible for backing up your data. Since the Website does not have access to any data, we can not help you recover any lost data.

3. Third-Party Services

The Website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to gain insights into website usage. This service does not track individual users and only provides aggregate data about page visits to help prioritize development. For more information, please refer to Cloudflare's web analytics privacy information: Cloudflare Web Analytics.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Website does not use cookies. We use localStorage to store your game progress data. LocalStorage is a type of web storage that allows websites to store data in your browser. This data is only accessible by you and remains on your device unless you choose to clear it. Do note that any third party extensions or plugins you have installed in your browser may be able to access this data. Read more about web storage on MDN Web Docs: Web Storage API.

5. User Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there are no personal data records for you to access, correct, or delete. Your game progress data is fully within your control, and you can manage it through your browser's storage settings or through your Dropbox account if you have opted to connect to Dropbox.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Although very unlikely, we may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective according to the date at the top of the page.
